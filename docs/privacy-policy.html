Privacy Policy

iClose · Last updated 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the iClose mobile app (“iClose”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information. If you choose to sign in, we process your email address and a unique account identifier through our authentication provider. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the email/identifier they return (you may use Apple’s “Hide My Email”).
  • Activity & preferences. Homes you save and anonymous interaction signals (views, skips, taps) used to personalise your feed. Saved homes are stored on your device; anonymous, non-identifying interaction events are stored to improve recommendations.
  • Enquiries you send. If you contact an agent (WhatsApp, call, or “book a viewing”), that action is handled by your device’s phone/WhatsApp and is not stored by us beyond an anonymous interaction event.
  • Technical data. Standard app/diagnostic data needed to operate and secure the service.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers, and we do not sell your personal data.

How we use information

  • To provide the app: show listings, save homes, and let you sign in.
  • To personalise your discovery feed.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

Service providers

We use Supabase for authentication and database/analytics storage, and Apple / Google for optional social sign-in. Listing imagery is served from a content delivery network. These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy terms.

Data retention

We keep account data while your account exists. Anonymous interaction events are retained only as long as needed to operate recommendations. You can delete saved homes any time in the app.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and associated data directly in the app: Profile → Delete account. This removes your authentication record and associated personal data and cannot be undone.

Children

iClose is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Your rights

Depending on your region you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us using the details below.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
